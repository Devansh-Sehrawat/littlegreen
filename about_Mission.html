Mission

Taking baby steps it has expanded its wings over the years, understanding, experiencing and loving children. Every effort is made to ensure that the first step of child’s education should be playful, stress free and full of smiles.

Besides playschool, Little Pearls offers a Daycare facility with the world class infrastructure and quality program for the children 0-10 years. The day care caters to the needs of all working parents who look for a home away from home for their child. It is ensured that the children do not miss the warmth and secuity of home and their physical, social and emotional needs are catered to as well.
